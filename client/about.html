SINCE 2019

We started with one espresso machine and a stubborn idea about freshness.

Coffee co. opened as a single counter with a two-week roast rotation. Six years later, the counter's bigger but the rule hasn't changed: nothing sits on a shelf.

Roasted in-house, twice a week

We roast Tuesdays and Fridays in small batches, so nothing on the menu is more than a few days off the drum.

Beans come from three family-run farms we buy from directly, at prices well above commodity market rate.

A short menu, on purpose

Fifteen drinks, no seasonal gimmicks. Every one earned its spot by being something our regulars actually order twice.

If something new joins the menu, something else has to leave first.
